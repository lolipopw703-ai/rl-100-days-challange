How to Analyze Replays & Take Notes

Step-by-Step Replay Review

  1. Save Replay: After ranked → Save Replay
  2. Watch in 0.25x Speed
  3. Ask:
    • What did I do wrong on defense?
    • Where was I out of position?
    • Did I waste boost or overcommit?
  4. Write It Below → Fix in Next Game
Pro Tip: Review 1 loss > 10 wins. Fix mistakes = rank up.
